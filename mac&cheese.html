Mac & Cheese

A dish that has a place in everyone's heart

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Kraft mac and cheese
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 tbsp of butter

Instructions

  1. Boil water and add macaroni
  2. Patiently wait for a couple of minutes
  3. Drain the water
  4. Add cheese powder, milk, and butter
