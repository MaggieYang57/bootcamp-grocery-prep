Turkey Sandwhich

The easiest, most reliable lunch you can imagine

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 3 turkey slices
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 cup of chopped tomato
  • 2 leaves of lettuce
  • 2 spoons of mayo

Instructions

  1. Lather both slices of bread up with the mayo
  2. Put the turkey, cheese, tomatoes, and lettuce on the mayo
