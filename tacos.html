Tacos

An absolute essential meal to have if it is Tuesday.

Goes great with guacamole or salsa!

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 5 tortillas
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 3 cups of shredded lettuce
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese
  • 3 scoops of sour cream

Instructions

  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Chop up the tomato
  3. Distribute ingredients among tacos
